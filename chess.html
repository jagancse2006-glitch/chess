♟ The Game of Chess

A timeless battle of strategy and intellect

Introduction

Chess is a two-player strategy board game that has been played for centuries. It is one of the most popular games in the world, enjoyed by millions of people of all ages.

Basic Rules

  • Chess is played on an 8x8 board with 64 squares.
  • Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 King, 1 Queen, 2 Rooks, 2 Knights, 2 Bishops, and 8 Pawns.
  • The goal is to checkmate your opponent's king.
  • Each piece moves differently, and strategy is key to winning.

Why People Love Chess

Chess improves critical thinking, memory, and patience. It is often called the "gymnasium of the mind" because it sharpens logical and strategic skills.
